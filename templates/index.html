PubSubJubhub

A little web service that allows you to subscribe to PubSubHubbub feeds from Javascript!

Query:

GET http://pubsubhubbub.appspot.com/?
 hub.url=http://blog.superfeedr.com&
 hub.callback=http://myapp.com/push/123&
 hub.mode=subscribe

Of course, you can also specify a hub.verify, a hub.lease_seconds, a hub.secret, a hub.verify_token...

And for all these feeds which do not have a designated hub, you can as well specify your superfeedr.login and superfeedr.password if you have a Superfeedr account.

Finally, You can as well add a callback parameter.

Response:

{"code":"200", "All Good!"}
